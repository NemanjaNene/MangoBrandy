The Craft

From Grove to Glass

01
Selection
Handpicked from family groves outside Puerto Plata

Only the finest sun-ripened Keitt and Kent mangoes make the cut. Each fruit is inspected at peak ripeness—when sugars and acidity reach perfect balance—ensuring exceptional aromatics in every batch.

100% Natural · Zero Additives
02
Fermentation
Small-batch natural fermentation

Fresh mango pulp ferments slowly over 10-14 days, developing complex flavor compounds. Temperature-controlled rooms maintain ideal conditions, allowing wild Caribbean yeasts to work their magic naturally.

10-14 Days · Controlled Environment
03
Distillation
Traditional copper pot stills

Double distillation in handcrafted copper alembic stills. The first run captures the essence; the second refines it to crystal clarity. Master distillers carefully separate heads, hearts, and tails—keeping only the purest center cut.

Heart Cut Only · Premium Quality
04
Proofing
Limestone-filtered Caribbean water

The spirit is gently proofed to 40% ABV using pure limestone-filtered water from local springs. This final step preserves the vibrant mango character while ensuring smooth, balanced sipping.

40% ABV · Crystal Clarity
